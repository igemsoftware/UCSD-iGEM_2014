Using The Application

  1. First enter your starting species/species into the input:
    You can do a single entry:
    OR
    Multiple entries should be separated by the word "and", with each entry being separated by a space:
  2. Enter your final species, following the same rules as the ones for starting species.
  3. You can also chose a direct or indirect path by checking of the box. The indirect path is a connection of disjoint paths by convergence for the production of the final output species.
  4. Finally press Explore and see your results.

What you will be able to see

  1. Network exploration of genetic devices
  2. Visuals of devices in SBOL format
  3. Information about devices and references to literature
  4. CAD for novel circuit design
  5. Centralized access to database of information
  6. Able to have user-contributed expansion to database
